
# Calculus

__Difference quotient__ &mdash; the slope of the __secant line__ of a function $f$, that is; $\frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}=\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}$

__Derivative / Differential Quotient__ &mdash; the slope of the __tangent line__ of a differentiable function $f$, that is; $\lim_{\Delta x \rightarrow 0} (\frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x})$, or equivalently $\frac{\delta f}{\delta x}=\underbrace{\frac{\delta y}{\delta x}}_{\text{Leibniz}}=\underbrace{\dot f}_{\text{Newton}}=\underbrace{f'}_{\text{Lagrange}}$


Derivative.__Sum rule__ &mdash; $(f+g)'=f'+g'$

Derivative.__Product rule__ &mdash; $(fg)'=f'g+g'f$

Derivative.__Quotient rule__ &mdash; $(f/g)'=\cfrac{f'g-g'f}{g^2}$

Derivative.__Chain rule__ &mdash; $(f(g))'=f'(g)g'$
