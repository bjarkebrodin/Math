
# Geometry

__Secant line__ &mdash; a line through $(x_0,f(x_0))$ and $(x_1,f(x_1))$ where $|x_1-x_0| > 0$ is a __secant line__ for the function $f$

__Tangent line__ &mdash; a line $ax+b$ through $(x_0,f(x_0))$ where $f'(x_0)=a$ is a __tangent line__ for $f$

__Euclidean distance__ &mdash; straight line distance, the distance between $a = \begin{bmatrix}x_0\\y_0\end{bmatrix}$ and $b = \begin{bmatrix}x_0\\y_0\end{bmatrix}$ is given by the magnitude of their difference $dist_{Euclidean}(a,b)=|a-b|$. This generalizes to $n$ dimensions too.

__Manhattan distance__ &mdash; taxi-cab distance, the distance between $a$ and $b$ is then $dist_{Manhattan}(a,b)=\begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1\end{bmatrix} \cdot \begin{bmatrix}|a_0-b_0| \\ |a_1-b_1|\end{bmatrix}$. Again, this generalizes to $n$ dimensions.
